#  User Funnel Analysis

##  Problem Statement

Businesses often struggle to understand where users drop off in their journey, leading to low conversion rates and missed revenue opportunities.

---

##  Objective

* Analyze user journey across funnel stages
* Calculate conversion rates and drop-offs
* Identify bottlenecks in the user journey

---

##  Tools Used

* Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
* Jupyter Notebook

---

##  Funnel Stages

**Visit → Signup → Purchase**

---

##  Funnel Overview

The funnel tracks user movement across key stages:

* Total users: **5000**
* Visit → Signup conversion: **~59%**
* Signup → Purchase conversion: **~37%**

This indicates strong acquisition but weak final conversion.

##  Funnel Visualization

![Funnel Chart] https://github.com/NuraMishra/Funnel_Analysis/blob/main/funnel%20graph.png

* Moderate drop from Visit to Signup
* Significant drop from Signup to Purchase (~63%)

This indicates a major conversion bottleneck.

---

##  Key Insights

* ~40% drop at signup stage → onboarding friction
* ~63% drop at purchase stage → poor conversion
* Acquisition is strong, but monetization is weak

---

##  Business Recommendations

* Improve signup experience
* Optimize checkout process
* Use retargeting strategies
* Offer incentives for conversion
